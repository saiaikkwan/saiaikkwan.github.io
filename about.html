About Me

Networking & Cybersecurity student building practical skills through hands-on learning

Hi, I'm Sai Aik Kwan

I'm a Networking and Cybersecurity student at Auston College, Myanmar, pursuing my diploma in partnership with the Auston Institute of Management, Singapore and my bachelor's degree in partnership with the University of Wolverhampton, UK. Based in Mandalay, I'm passionate about building secure and efficient network infrastructures.

My journey into networking began with a curiosity about how data travels across the internet. That curiosity evolved into a dedicated homelab where I practice enterprise-level networking concepts, security configurations, and system administration.

I'm currently preparing for CCNA and CompTIA Security+ certifications while documenting my learning through practical projects on GitHub.
